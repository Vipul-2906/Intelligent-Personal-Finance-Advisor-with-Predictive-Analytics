💡 Why Build an Emergency Fund?

  • Covers 3–6 months of living expenses if you lose your job or face an unexpected crisis.
  • Prevents you from going into debt for emergencies.
  • Provides peace of mind and financial resilience.
  • Start small — even ₹500/month adds up to ₹6,000/year.
  • Keep it in a separate, liquid savings account.
